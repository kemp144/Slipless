## Recommended Story Order

Your screenshots should sell the app in this order:

1. Home screen
2. Urge or recovery action screen
3. Progress screen
4. History screen
5. Settings or privacy screen

Why this order works:

- First image explains the core product fast.
- Second image shows emotional value in the hardest moment.
- Third image proves measurable progress.
- Fourth image shows depth and honesty.
- Fifth image closes with privacy and trust.

## Design Notes

- Keep headline text short, ideally 3 to 5 words.
- Keep subheadline text to one line if possible.
- Put text in the upper third of the image.
- Use the same font, spacing, and alignment across all 5 images.
- Avoid overcrowding the first screenshot; it carries the most conversion weight.
- Do not mention widgets, subscriptions, or cloud sync.

## Practical Tip

If one screenshot is visually weaker than the others, replace it rather than trying to save it with text. In App Store conversion, weak screenshot 1 or 2 hurts far more than having only 4 strong images.
