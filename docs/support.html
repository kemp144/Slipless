Slipless Support

Need help with Slipless?

What to include

  • Your device model
  • Your iOS version
  • The app version you are using
  • A short description of the problem

Privacy

Slipless stores data locally on your device and does not require an account. Read the full policy on the privacy page.

Getting help

For public support, open an issue at github.com/robertengel/Slipless/issues. Include the device, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.

App Review

If you are on the App Review team and need clarification about app behavior, use the Support URL listed in App Store Connect together with the review notes provided by the developer.
